    DETAILS
    • Top handcrafted of white oak veneers and kiln-dried engineered wood
    • Brass base is hand-cast solid brass with an antiqued finish; Pewter base is hand-cast aluminum with an antiqued finish; Bronze base is hand-cast brass with an antiqued Bronze finish
    • Solid metal table base is hand cast by skilled artisans employing an ancient foundry technique known in French as cire perdue (lost-wax). The base is individually sculpted by pouring molten metal into a custom wax mold. As the wax melts away, the finished piece emerges.
    • Oak is wire brushed to highlight its rich grain; a sealed wax finish enhances its color
    • Metal has an antiqued finish with visible highs and lows
