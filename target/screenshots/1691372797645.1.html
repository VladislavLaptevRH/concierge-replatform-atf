    DETAILS
    • Handcrafted of kiln-dried solid oak with white oak veneers
    • Oak is wire brushed to highlight its rich grain; a sealed wax finish enhances its color
    • Brass hardware is hand-cast solid brass with an antiqued finish; Pewter hardware is hand-cast aluminum with an antiqued finish; Bronze hardware is hand-cast brass with an antiqued Bronze finish
    • Solid metal hardware is hand cast by skilled artisans employing an ancient foundry technique known in French as cire perdue (lost-wax). The hardware is individually sculpted by pouring molten metal into a custom wax mold. As the wax melts away, the finished piece emerges.
    • Hand-cast corner brackets feature exposed screws
    • Wood slat support; requires a mattress and foundation
    • A low-profile foundation (sold separately) is recommended for use with a foam-based mattress
    • King and Cal King bed frames are compatible with our FreshBed™ Sleep System
    • This bed frame accommodates our Reverie® Adjustable Base. Base will not be visible above side rails.
    • Please note that our delivery team is unable to install this item with adjustable bases other than our Reverie Adjustable Base. Contact us at 800.762.1005 for information about compatibility with other bases.
    • This item is artisan crafted with meticulous care. Given its handmade and hand-finished nature, variations in the wood or metal work are to be expected and celebrated. Each item is unique and no two are exactly alike.
    • Wipe with a soft, dry cloth; avoid the use of harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaning materials. Avoid direct sunlight and heat to protect the finish.
