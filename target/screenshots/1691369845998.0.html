    DETAILS
    • Handcrafted of kiln-dried hardwood with corner-blocked joinery
    • Low-slung platform design with all-around 4"-wide, sumptuously padded rail
    • King and Cal King beds without storage are compatible with our FreshBed Sleep System; FreshBed Sleep System is not compatible with storage option
    • Bed without storage accommodates our Reverie Adjustable Base; base will be visible above side rails. Reverie Adjustable Base is not recommended with storage option.
    • Stocked Fabrics (48" and 60" Headboard Heights) and Special Order Fabrics (36", 72", 84" and 96" Headboard Heights): Bed without storage features wood slat support and is designed for use with a mattress only; a low-profile foundation (sold separately) is recommended when using a foam-based mattress with our non-storage bed.
    • Special Order Fabrics (48" and 60" Headboard Heights): Adjustable wood slat support with 2 height options: lower setting requires mattress and box spring; higher setting for use with a mattress only; a low-profile foundation (sold separately) is recommended when using a foam-based mattress with our non-storage bed.
    • Storage option features 1 large fully finished drawer at foot of bed with 2 interior compartments; drawer slides on heavy-duty casters
    • Choice of headboard panel height in 1-foot increments ranging from 36"H to 96"H
    • Headboard is generously padded with high-resiliency foam
    • Back of headboard is fully upholstered, allowing versatility in room placement
    • 84"H and 96"H headboards require securing to the wall with cleat hardware (included)
    • Assembly provided upon delivery. RH delivery service does not secure the 84"H and 96"H headboards to the wall; professional installation required.
    • Please note that our delivery team is unable to install this item with adjustable bases other than our Reverie Adjustable Base. Contact us at 800.762.1005 for information about compatibility with other bases.
    • Professional upholstery cleaning recommended. Vacuum with an upholstery attachment to remove dust.
    • Wipe wood with a soft, dry cloth; avoid the use of harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaning materials. Avoid direct sunlight and heat to protect the finish.
