    Designed exclusively for us by Reverie®, an industry leader in adjustable sleep systems since 2003, our base adjusts with separate upper and lower-body controls for a fully customizable sleep position. Engineered for smooth, quiet operation, it lets sleepers select the perfect position to promote healthy posture and proper circulation, and relieves pressure on the neck, back and joints for optimal comfort and a sound night's sleep.

    View In-Stock Options
    DETAILS
    • Designed exclusively for RH by Reverie to pair seamlessly with our bed collections
    • Remote control lets you raise and lower head and feet to a virtually unlimited number of positions; includes 3 preset positions (zero gravity, anti-snore and flat) and 2 programmable custom positions
    • King and Cal-King sizes each feature a split base that allows independent movement when paired with a split mattress, or simultaneous movement with any mattress
    • Compatible with all RH mattresses and most bed frames; refer to bed product details for compatibility
    • May be used with a foam-based or innerspring mattress; when used with an innerspring mattress, the mattress will sit higher when weight is not applied
    • 8"H legs allow for an elevated base on standard bed frames that have a removable slat support system
    • Base can be used with an existing support system by removing legs and will then sit higher in bed frame; if additional height is required, base may be paired with a low-profile foundation
    • Upholstered in light grey fabric
    • Backlit wireless remote (included)
    • 850 lb. weight limit
    • 20-Year Limited Warranty offered exclusively through Reverie
    • Final Sale: All mattresses, foundations and bases are non-returnable once packaging is removed
